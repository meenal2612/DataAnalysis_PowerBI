# DataAnalysis_PowerBI
<h2><img align="center" height="80" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/70b1437c-b9fd-4b27-9f2a-f7d567a6199d"> Customer Insights & Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI) </h2>
This project showcases an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer behavior and track sales performance. The dashboard provides:

1. Customer Insights: Segmentation, purchase trends, and retention analysis.
2. Sales Performance: Revenue growth, product-wise sales, and regional comparisons.
3. KPIs & Visualizations: Clear metrics for decision-making, including dynamic charts and filters.
4. Actionable Analytics: Helps identify top-performing products, customer preferences, and areas for improvement.
  
🎯Purpose

The Customer Insights & Sales Performance Dashboard empowers businesses to make data-driven decisions by visualizing customer behavior, tracking sales trends, and highlighting key performance indicators. Built in Power BI, it transforms raw data into actionable insights that improve strategy, optimize sales, and enhance customer engagement.

<img align="center" height="15" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9271580c-4ac2-4f65-97dd-b15ff3eb6c22"> Features / Highlights
 
 - Business Problem: Modern businesses generate vast amounts of customer and sales data, yet managers, analysts, and decision-makers often lack an intuitive way to consolidate insights across regions, products, and customer segments. Without a unified dashboard, identifying growth opportunities, tracking performance, and understanding customer behavior becomes fragmented and inefficient.
 - Key questions such as: Who is the top-performing salesperson? Which city is generating the maximum revenue? what is the growth rate YOY? … are difficult to answer quickly with raw data.
 -  The goal of the Customer Insights & Sales Performance Dashboard is to provide an interactive tool that unifies customer and sales data into clear visual insights.
It enables businesses to explore customer segments, track sales trends, and evaluate regional performance in real time.
By uncovering patterns in behavior, growth, and retention, the dashboard supports smarter decisions in product strategy, marketing, and resource allocation.
